About SB DA-IICT


The Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers Student Branch at DA-IICT endeavors to promote awareness among students on the opportunities that exist in the field of ICT . Our objective is to bridge the gap between industry and students studying in technology-related fields, while promoting and providing an intellectually stimulating university environment for the student body.

IEEE Student Branch, DA-IICT aims to provide an interactive platform for students to develop professional and technical abilities. Since the inception, it is successful in organizing various events like workshops , seminars , conferences with well defined objectives to encourage youth for technological enhancements and hope to continue the same. IEEE student branch daiict has been winning the best student branch award in Gujarat section since past 2 years .

About DA-IICT


Established in Gandhinagar, Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology represents a perfect model for Wave – 4 educational innovation in Gujarat. With its primary focus on higher education through maximum participation from technology itself, DA-IICT has maintained its reputation of being one of the best engineering universities of India. DA-IICT, promoted by Dhirubhai Ambani Foundation and Anil Dhirubhai Ambani Group is a private university, included in the list of the universities maintained by University Grants Commission (UGC). DA-IICT was started with a vision to provide India with a research oriented institute that matches international standards and produces pioneers in the field of Information and Communication Technology. From its very inception, the university has been a centre for revolutionary research and innovations and a juncture for some of the greatest minds to collaborate in their quest for advancement of science and technology.

With a functional architecture, surrounded by fascinating flora and fauna, environment friendly design and a landscape planned in such a way that it conserves excess of natural resources - the campus itself is an example of what it wants to achieve – innovations through engineering.
