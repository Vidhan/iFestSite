I.Fest 2012






I.Fest





I.Fest was born out of the motive to celebrate 125 years of IEEE. The main aim of the fest is to act as a platform for spreading knowledge about science and technology with fun. It provides an opportunity to learn new things and applying the things learnt to solve some existing problems in the real world. The events that will be conducted over the two days will make you think beyond the limits, let your imagination go to newer places, bring out your creative skills and in the end leave you wanting for more. With a range of events from the field of electronics to computer science and to know what the eminent personalities have to say about the current things that are happening around, i.fest will give perfect platform to excel in the field of science and technology. Besides giving you the opportunity to compete with your peers, i.fest also provides a common platform to students from all over India to share their experiences and knowledge with their fellow IEEE members.

Anticipation, exhilaration and exuberance are all a part of this fest. So, be a part of this extravaganza which offers a junction of fun, technology, and wisdom.
